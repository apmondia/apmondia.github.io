About Taliflo

Taliflo was founded by Anthony Mondia, CPA in 2013 with the vision of developing and bringing peer-to-peer financial technology to the market. Early prototypes were tested with business and non-profit networks, but failed to overcome apprehension towards new and untested technologies. The startup was put on hold from 2016 to 2022 while blockchain technology became more accepted by the market and Anthony gained valuable experience as a software product manager and non-profit director.
